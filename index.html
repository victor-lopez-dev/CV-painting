Meet the Owner

Carlos Terry – Owner of C&V Painting Services

With over 20 years of painting experience, Carlos Terry founded C&V Painting Services with a simple goal: deliver high-quality craftsmanship while treating every home with care and respect. Carlos believes that great painting starts with proper preparation, attention to detail, and clear communication with every client. From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, he is committed to making sure every project reflects the quality and professionalism the company stands for. Under Carlos's leadership, C&V Painting Services has built a reputation for reliable service, beautiful finishes, and results homeowners can trust.
